Documenting the History of UFO Sightings in New Zealand

In 2010, the New Zealand Ministry of Defence made copies of their UFO documents open to the public, albeit with details redacted. Though the original files remain restricted in the New Zealand Archives until further notice, the public version of the documents reveal enough to tell a strange yet interesting aspect of New Zealand history. This site aims to make the information and history of these files more accessible through select reports.

Though the UFO documents can be requested at the National Library of New Zealand, they can be easily accessed on the Internet Archives.
